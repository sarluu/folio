SPEAK: A DIGITAL MINI-ZINE

SPEAK is a mini-zine digitally made with alienmelon's Electric Zine Maker. The work contains pieces that showcase my childhood experiences learning my family's native languages (Mandarin and Vietnamese). Communication barriers and the alienation of being an "ex-prodigy" are part of a few topics that are covered in this zine in relation to language learning.
